Plant Care Prototype - Components
=================================

ESP32 Dev Board - Rs 450 (Robu.in)
Capacitive soil moisture sensor - Rs 150 (Amazon)
DHT22 temperature/humidity sensor - Rs 120 (local electronics shop)
Relay module - Rs 70 (Amazon)
Mini pump - Rs 200 (Amazon, 3-6V DC)
Float switch - Rs 100 (Amazon)
Jumpers + breadboard - already available
USB cable + 5V adapter - reused phone charger

Total: approx Rs 1,090

Notes:
- The first resistive soil probe corroded quickly, so it was replaced with a capacitive sensor.
- DHT22 was selected to match firmware and simulation sensor assumptions.
